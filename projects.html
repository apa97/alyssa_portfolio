FEATURED PROJECTS

Munchie Voice-Based Mobile App

For this app, we designed a voice-activated food app to support university students during times where they have tight budgets and busy schedules. The app helps our users discover affordable ingredients in their area and suggests simple recipes based on what they already have at home or want to buy. The goal and focus was the accessibility, ease of use, and solving a real problem for students.

A Mini-Arcade Quartet

This interactive project was built using Arduino and featured four custom-coded mini-games, for example included a memory game, a reflex-speed challenge and more. Players needed to complete all four games in order to unlock a final prize (a chocolate coin that would slide down in a makeshift coinslot to the player). Our goal was to create a fun, engaging experience by using Arduino and creating design elements similar to an arcade.
